# Entrance

Key required for access. Log book located just inside.

**Golden Rule for Entrance Series:** Always take the left-hand option at junctions.

## Terrain
- Sporting boulders
- Narrow rifts
- Short crawls and climbs
- ~500m long passage

> [!WARNING]
> Avoid high-level passages - these are bat roosting sites.
