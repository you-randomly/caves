# The Music Room

The destination chamber, easily identifiable by the **music stand** in the middle.

## Onward Routes

### North Wing
Head **left** at the music stand through a low arch to reach:
- **Cliffs of Dover** - Multi-strata sand and clay banks
- **Ace of Spades Streamway** - Located in a canyon straight ahead before the main passage swings left

### Aven Series
- **The Large Cairn** marks the junction with Aven Series (access via crawl to the right)
- **Aven Series Chamber** contains a display of mud sculptures
- **Route 63** - Accessed via a maypole climb above the cairn
