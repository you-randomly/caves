# Second Inlet

Located **250m** beyond First Inlet in Southern Stream Passage.

## Navigation
- Beyond this point, navigation becomes more difficult
- Route involves crawling and a bypass for a low roof
- Leads to Waterfall Chamber

## Route Context
Part of the **Grand Circle** and **Iles Inlet** routes.
