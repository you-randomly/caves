# Draught Passage Junction

## Key Navigation Points

1. **The Smooth Hole** - Located at a taped-off section/sign. Descend here through boulders on the right.
2. **Draught Passage** - At the end of the boulder section, descend right.
3. **Left-hand side passage** - Low down, immediately after Draught Passage. This is crucial for route finding!
