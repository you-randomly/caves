# High Traverse

A dramatic balcony traverse with a fixed line, marking the transition from Gothic Passage area to the Main Streamway.

## Features
- Balcony overlooking the streamway below
- Fixed line for safety
- Notable landmark: **Inflatable skeleton decoration**

## Tackle Required
- **Cowstails recommended** for the fixed line

## Navigation
- Descent from here leads into the Main Streamway
- Part of the Grand Circle clockwise route

> [!WARNING]
> Use the fixed line for safety on this exposed traverse.
