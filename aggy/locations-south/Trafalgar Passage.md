# Trafalgar Passage

A beautifully decorated passage with well-preserved formations, accessed beyond the 2005 breakthrough in Iles Inlet.

## Features
- Pristine formations
- Protected environment
- One of the most decorated areas in Aggy

## Navigation
- Accessed from [[Iles Inlet]]
- Side-rift leads to [[Corkscrew Chamber]] and The Courtesan

> [!WARNING]
> Take extreme care to preserve the delicate formations in this area.
