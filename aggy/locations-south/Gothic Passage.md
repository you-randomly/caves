# Gothic Passage

A large sandy passage accessed via a rope climb and traverse from Southern Stream Passage.

## Features
- Begins as easy walking passage
- Lowers to a crawl further along
- Sandy floor sections

## Navigation
- **Left at T-junction**: Leads to Priory Road (for Iles Inlet route)
- **Continue**: Leads to Sandstone Passage and Synchronicity

## Onward Routes
- [[Priory Road]] - Prolonged crawl to Iles Inlet and The Courtesan
- [[Synchronicity]] - Large sandy chamber leading to High Traverse
