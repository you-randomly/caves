# Waterfall Chamber

A chamber featuring a **2m waterfall** with a fixed handline for descent.

## Features
- 2m waterfall drop
- Fixed handline for safety
- Fluted rock formations near the waterfall

## Navigation
- Rope climb and traverse leads to Gothic Passage
- Part of the route to Priory Road and Iles Inlet
