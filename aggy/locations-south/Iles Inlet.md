# Iles Inlet

A passage section featuring deep pits, excavations, and sand squeezes, leading to beautifully decorated passage.

## Features
- Deep pits and excavations
- Sand squeeze sections
- 2005 breakthrough point where well-preserved formations begin

## Navigation
- Continues from Priory Road
- Leads to [[Trafalgar Passage]] with its pristine formations
- Side-rift on left leads to [[Corkscrew Chamber]]

## Route Context
Named route to **The Courtesan**, one of the finest formations in Aggy.

> [!TIP]
> The 2005 breakthrough opened access to remarkably well-preserved formations beyond.
