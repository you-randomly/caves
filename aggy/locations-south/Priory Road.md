# Priory Road

A prolonged crawl passage accessed from Gothic Passage, leading toward Iles Inlet and The Courtesan.

## Features
- Extended low crawl sections
- Contains Rock Hard Camp landmark
- Inlet passage partway along

## Navigation
- **Left at Gothic Passage T-junction** to reach Priory Road
- Continue through the crawl to reach [[Iles Inlet]]
- Diversion available to [[The Cathedral]] via aven tube
