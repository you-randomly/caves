# Corkscrew Chamber

A decorated chamber accessed via a side-rift from Trafalgar Passage, home to **The Courtesan** - the finest formation in Agen Allwedd.

## Features
- **The Courtesan** - Premier formation in the cave system
- Various urchin formations
- Delicate speleothems

## Access
- Side-rift on the left from Trafalgar Passage
- Part of the Iles Inlet route

> [!CAUTION]
> This chamber contains the most prized formations in Aggy. Take extreme care and do not touch any formations.
