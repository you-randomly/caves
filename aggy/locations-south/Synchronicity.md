# Synchronicity

A large sandy chamber accessed from Gothic Passage, serving as a junction point on the Grand Circle route.

## Features
- Large open chamber
- Sandy floor
- Junction with multiple passage options

## Navigation
- From here, a taped route leads to the High Traverse balcony
- Connection to Resurrection Passage (for Maytime route)

## Route Context
Key waypoint on the **Grand Circle** clockwise route.
